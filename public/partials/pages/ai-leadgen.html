08 · You're Probably Wondering

The questions everyone asks (answered honestly).

Isn't this just spam? Won't it hurt my domain?

No — and that's the entire reason we charge what we charge. We use dedicated sending domains separate from your main one, warm them properly for 2–3 weeks before any volume, run real personalization (not mail-merge tokens), and stay well under safe daily limits. Your primary domain reputation never sees the campaign. Most "AI outreach" tools blow up domains. We've been doing this for years without a single client losing deliverability.

What counts as a "qualified meeting"?

A real prospect from your defined ICP, with budget authority, who showed up to the call. We define the criteria together in week one — title, company size, industry, intent signals — and the AI only books prospects who match. If a meeting doesn't qualify (wrong person, no-show, junk), it doesn't count toward your number.

What if it doesn't deliver 30 meetings in 60 days?

We keep working for free until it does. No additional setup fee. No additional monthly fee. The reason we can offer this is we've run this playbook 100+ times — we know what works for B2B service, SaaS, agencies, and local businesses. If your campaign isn't hitting, that's our problem to solve, not your bill to pay.

Will the emails sound like AI wrote them?

No. The AI reads the prospect's LinkedIn, their company news, their recent posts, and their tech stack — then writes an opener that references something specific and current. Reply rates run 3–5x generic templates because the prospect feels seen, not blasted. We also have a copywriter review tone before launch.

Does this replace my sales team?

It replaces the prospecting half of their job — not the closing half. Your team should be in deep conversations, running demos, and closing deals. Not building lists, scraping LinkedIn, and writing 80 emails a day. We free them from the soul-crushing top-of-funnel work so they can do what they're actually good at.

How fast does this start working?

Week 1: training and infrastructure. Week 2: campaigns launch, first replies come in. Week 3: meetings start booking. By week 4 most clients see consistent flow. By month 2 you should be at 30+ qualified meetings. Month 3 onward typically beats month 2 because the model keeps learning.
