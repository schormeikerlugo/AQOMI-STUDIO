Project

China Southern Airlines

We worked with China Southern Airlines on a series of holiday campaigns for their brand. They asked us to incorporate Chinese lanterns — like the ones lit at festivals — into the key visuals.

The campaign ran across their website for several months, reaching millions of visitors worldwide and delivering a visual experience that honoured the cultural tradition behind the imagery.
