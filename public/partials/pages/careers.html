What we offer

Direct mentorship from a globally recognised creative director. Exposure to projects spanning 40 countries. A studio that operates at the highest standard --- and expects the same from everyone in it.

Who we hire

People who are obsessed with craft. Who find mediocrity physically uncomfortable. Who would rather do something once, perfectly, than quickly and again. If that sounds like you, read below.

Full-Time · Remote / Calgary Senior Art Director
Open +

You are not a production artist. You are a conceptual thinker who can execute at the highest level. You will own entire brand systems --- from the first strategic insight to the final pixel --- and will be expected to challenge the brief at every step.

Responsibilities
  • Lead visual identity development on 3---5 concurrent brand projects
  • Direct photography, video, and DreamLab production sessions
  • Translate brand strategy into visual language with rigour and originality
  • Present and defend creative decisions to clients at senior level
  • Maintain AQOMI's standard of quality across every touchpoint
Requirements
  • 7+ years in brand identity or visual design at agency level
  • Portfolio demonstrating conceptual range and executional precision
  • Expert-level Adobe Creative Suite; Figma; motion a plus
  • Deep understanding of typography, colour theory, and hierarchy
  • Ability to work with extreme independence
