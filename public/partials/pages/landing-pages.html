08 · You're Probably Wondering

The questions everyone asks (answered honestly).

Seven days. Really? That sounds too fast.

It's fast because we removed everything that slows agencies down — the kickoff theater, the project management overhead, the open calendar. We block the week, work on nothing else, and ship by Friday. The only thing that delays us is when you take three weeks to send your logo. As long as you're responsive, we're done in seven.

What if it turns out terrible?

You walk away. You pay nothing. Not the setup. Not the monthly. We'd genuinely rather lose the deal than build something you don't believe in — which is why we have a #1 Clutch rating and not a folder full of unhappy clients.

Why charge a setup fee AND a monthly?

The $2,500 covers the build week — discovery, design, development, launch. The $250/month covers what comes after: hosting, SSL, security, analytics, AND unlimited revisions for as long as you stay. Most agencies charge you for every change you ask for. We don't. The page evolves with your business.

What if I want out later?

Cancel any time. The domain stays in your name from day one — we just hold the keys. We'll help you migrate the site to a new host if you're leaving. No lock-in. No exit fee. No drama.

How are you this much cheaper than a real agency?

We don't bill by the hour. We don't have account managers, project managers, and creative directors all stacked on one project. The scope is finite — one page, seven days, a defined product. What's left is the work itself, executed at studio standard. That's why $2,500 + $250/month is possible without cutting corners.

Do you write the words for me?

By default, you bring the copy. If writing isn't your thing, we'll quote a copywriting add-on during the discovery call. Most clients have their messaging closer to ready than they think — we'll know in 30 minutes.
